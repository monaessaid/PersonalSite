Hello! My name is Mona Es-Said.

I am an aspiring front end web developer graduating with a Master's in Software Development from the University of Glasgow in December 2023.

I have a Bachelors of Business Administration in Marketing and a range of work experience in nonprofits, marketing, events, IT, and human resources. I look forward to a new set of opportunities and challenges as a web developer.

About This Site

This is my portfolio website. I made it in September 2023 with HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, and Three.js for the 3D background. The planets move as you scroll through the page. A different part of the site will show the original model that you can interact with in three dimensions!
